Crystal Collector

Instructions:

  • You will be assigned a random number at the start of the game.
  • Click one of the crystals below to add an unknown amount to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the number assigned. You lose the game if your total number exceeds the value of the random number assigned.
  • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.
  • After every game, the values of the crystals will mysterically change!
